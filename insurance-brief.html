About This Brief
What it covers
Strategic-level trends in commercial lines and non-life insurance — market cycles, emerging risks, AI deployment, and regulatory shifts. Not policy-level detail. Business implications.

Consulting firms, insurers, and risk professionals who want a concise, opinionated read on what is actually moving in the market — not a summary of what everyone already knows.
